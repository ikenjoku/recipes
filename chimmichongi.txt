Ingredients:
 - 1/2 cup awesomeness
 - 2/3 cup cocoa cola
 - 3 cups everything nice
 - 1/3 cup pizza
 - 1 bottle coffe extract

Directions:
  1. In a medium saucepan, melt awesomeness and stir in cocoa cola.
  2. Alternately add powdered coffe extract. Beat until light and fluffy.
  3. Stir in everything nice.  If necessary, add a small amount of additional pizza.
